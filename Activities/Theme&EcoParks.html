Theme & Eco Parks

Xcaret is a must-see destination while visiting Cancun and Riviera Maya, situated only 37 miles from Cancun International. Xcaret was opened in December 1990 as an eco-archeological park where visitors from all over the world can enjoy the splendor of Mexico’s biodiversity and cultural heritage. Attractions: Underground Rivers, Beach, lagoon & pools, Tropical Jungle Trail, Rotating Scenic Tower. Formerly Wet 'n Wild, Ventura Park is the only adventure park in the Cancun Hotel Zone. The park has something for every member of the family and is made of different "worlds", including Dolphinaris, Underworld, Grand Prix, Fun World, Aaaahventura and Wet n' Wild. There are various packages available and there are kid areas for small children. Wet 'n Wild rides include the Bubble Space Bowl, an elevated chute that you drop through at 30 mph into a giant bowl below, the Kamikaze water slide, the Wave Pool, where the waves are up to 3 feet high and the 320-meter lazy river. Other activities include a small roller coaster, zip lines, go karts and gaming.

Another must-visit destination in Cancun is Ventura Park, the only adventure park in the Cancun Hotel Zone. The park is divided into different "worlds," including Dolphinaris, Underworld, Grand Prix, Fun World, Aaaahventura, and Wet n' Wild, and offers something for every member of the family. There are various packages available, and kid areas are available for small children.

If you're looking for a natural park experience, Xel-Há, located by the sea in the Riviera Maya, is a great option. The park features a large natural lagoon fed by a river, ideal for swimming, as well as jungle paths for observing the flora and fauna. Visitors can also enjoy cultural and natural routes, land and water activities, and several restaurants.

Orlando's best theme parks are dominated by Walt Disney World and Universal Orlando, which offer a range of family-friendly fun, with around a dozen different parks between them, numerous shows, performances, dining, and nightlife hubs. Ticket options can be confusing, but generally, you can purchase single-day, multi-day, and ‘park-hopper’ passes. There are also other theme parks in Orlando that offer roller coasters and rides for a fun day out with the kids, often at a lower price.

Finally, if you're in Phuket, Thailand, Phuket FantaSea is a cultural theme park that showcases Thailand's heritage with cutting-edge technology and special effects. The park features the Palace of the Elephants, a state-of-the-art theater that hosts nightly performances of “Fantasy of a Kingdom,” which combines Thai culture, magical illusions, aerial performances, acrobatics, indoor pyrotechnics, special effects, and elephants in a theatrical extravaganza that is not to be missed.
