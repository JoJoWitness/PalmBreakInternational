Orlando

Orlando FL - United States

Orlando is the largest city in central Florida, with more than 2 million people in its surrounding metropolitan area. The city is also home to several world-renowned tourist attractions, including Walt Disney World, Universal Studios and SeaWorld. No matter your reasons for visiting Orlando -- they could be related to business, pleasure or a combination of the two -- travel to the city using any of several means, including by car, plane, bus or train.
